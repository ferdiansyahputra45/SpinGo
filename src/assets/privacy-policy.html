Privacy Policy for SpinGo

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Data Collection

SpinGo does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data (items list, spin history) are stored locally on your device using localStorage.

Permissions

SpinGo does not require any special permissions.

Third-Party Services

SpinGo uses canvas-confetti library for celebration effects. No data is sent to any server.

Contact

If you have questions, contact: spingo@example.com
